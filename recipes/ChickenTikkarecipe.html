Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is made up 2 parts of an ingredients list. One is the actual marination of the chicken and the other is sauce.

    Ingredients List for chicken marination:
  • Raw chicken (marinated)
  • Onions
  • 1 or 2 teaspoons of cumin
  • 2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of red chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons of ground cumin
  • 3-6 cloves garlic, crushed or minced
  • 1/2 to 1 cup of yogurt
    Ingredient List for sauce
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 or 2 chopped Onions
  • 3 garlics, minced or chopped
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
