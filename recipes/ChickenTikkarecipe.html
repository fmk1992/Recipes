Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is made up 2 parts of an ingredients list. One is the actual marination of the chicken and the other is sauce.

    Ingredients List for chicken marination:
  • Raw chicken (marinated)
  • Onions
  • 1 or 2 teaspoons of cumin
  • 2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of red chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons of ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • chopped or crushed ginger
  • 3-6 cloves garlic, crushed or minced
  • 1/2 to 1 cup of yogurt
  • Lemon juice
    Ingredient List for sauce
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 or 2 chopped Onions
  • 3 garlics, minced or chopped
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 2 teaspoons of ground coriander
  • 2 teaspoons of chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato puree
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • cooked rice or naan bread for serving
    Instructions:
  1. Slice chicken into bite-sized chunks.
  2. Add yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, ginger, salt, cumin, and paprika and stir well.
  3. Cook until chicken is golden or cooked thoroughly, then set aside.
  4. Heat oil onto a pan.
  5. Add onions, ginger, and garlic until brown or tender.
  6. Add cumin, turmeric, coriander, paprika, and chili powder and stir.
  7. Stir and add in tomato puree, tomato sauce and 1 cup of water. Make sure to bring to a boil and cook for 5 minutes.
  8. Add the cream.
  9. Remove the chicken and add into the sauce for at least 2 minutes.
  10. Add cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
